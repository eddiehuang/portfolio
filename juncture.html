Juncture

Juncture is an app that helps you get together with your indecisive friends. This app was inspired by my friends who always want to get together, but never know what to do when weâ€™re together.

Demo

Problem

You have friends that want to get together but they can never decide on where to go. They have preferences but for whatever reason, they are having a hard time deciding on where to go. This makes planning and picking a place difficult.

Solution

Juncture takes into account all of your friends' preferences and gives you recommendations on where to go. It makes planning a bit easier.
